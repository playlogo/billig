Update steps:
1. Format the TF card, copy the Motherboard firmware (firmware.bin) to the root directory of the TF card, and only store this file in the TF card.

2. Insert the TF card into the printer in the shutdown state, and then turn on the printer.

3. Wait for a while, and automatically update the firmware in the black screen state.

4. After the update is completed, the screen will appear  “Anycubic LOGO”, and then automatically enter the main interface. Then shut down and pull out the SD card. update completed.
